Family Tree Required

A family tree account is required in order to create a new GenQuizitive Live game. By logging into FamilySearch you agree to allow GenQuizitive to read your family tree data.

When you create a GenQuizitive Live game, your game will be given a game ID. Only those with whom you share this game ID will be able to join your game and see your family tree data.

Click the "Connect Family Tree" button below to connect with FamilySearch and begin setting up your GenQuizitive Live game.
