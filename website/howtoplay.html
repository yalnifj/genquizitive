How to Play!

Getting Started - Practice GenQuiz - Challenge a Friend - Continue GenQuiz - Hints

 

Getting Started!

When you first come to the game you will be asked to login with your Facebook and/or your FamilySearch accounts.

A Facebook account is required to challenge your friends and family members, to play with hints, and to track your score and access leaderboards. When you connect GenQuizitive to your Facebook account, we will use your account to uniquely identify you and access your list of friends. You will be asked for permission anytime something is shared with one of your friends or posted to your Facebook feed.

A FamilySearch account is required to play with your personal family history. If you do not connect to a FamilySearch account, then you may only play GenQuiz rounds in which others have challenged you.

After you login, you will be shown the game's main menu. Choose from one of the road signs to begin playing.

Practice GenQuiz

The practice option allows you to play a GenQuiz round without challenging someone else. A FamilySearch account is required to practice a GenQuiz.

After clicking "Start" you will be presented with a question about your genealogy. Click here to learn more about our question types. After answering this question correctly, you will be presented with another slightly more difficult question. If you incorrectly answer a multiple choice question you will be given a penalty and a penalty light will turn on. Each penalty will add 20 seconds to your total GenQuiz time. The gauge shows how far you have progressed through the current GenQuiz round.

The GenQuiz round ends after you correctly answer 5 questions. You will then be shown your GenQuiz score. Each question will be shown and the time it took you to answer that question. You can learn more about the ancestor targeted in each question by clicking the ancestor badge next to the question. This opens a window with all of the family tree details about that person.

Challenge a Friend

The option to "Challenge a Friend" will allow you to start a GenQuiz with one of your Facebook friends. A FamilySearch account and a Facebook account are required.

You will first be shown a screen where you may choose from one of your Facebook friends who are also playing GenQuizitive. Click on one of the friend badges to start a new GenQuiz with them. To invite any of your other Facebook friends to play with you, click on the "Invite Friends" button and search for the friend you would like to start a new GenQuiz with.

After clicking "Start" you will be presented with a question about your genealogy. Click here to learn more about our question types. After answering this question correctly, you will be presented with another slightly more difficult question. If you incorrectly answer a multiple choice question you will be given a penalty and a penalty light will turn on. Each penalty will add 20 seconds to your total GenQuiz time. The gauge shows how far you have progressed through the current GenQuiz round.

After you complete the GenQuiz, your friend will receive a Facebook request letting them know that it is their turn. If your friend is logged into their FamilySearch account, then they will play with the same questions but the data will be from their family tree. If a question you answered targets a person that they also have in their family tree, then they will play with that person. If they have not logged in to a family tree account, then they will see the same questions that you answered with the data from your family tree. Questions will not target living people if you are playing with someone who does not have their own family tree account.

The GenQuiz round ends after you correctly answer 5 questions. You will then be shown your GenQuiz score. If your friend has also completed this GenQuiz then their score will also be shown. Each question will be shown and the time it took for you to answer that question. Each question will also show an ancestor badge for the person who was the target of that question. You can learn more about this ancestor by clicking on their ancestor badge. This open a window with all of the family tree details about that person.

Continue GenQuiz

The "Continue GenQuiz" option shows you the GenQuiz rounds that you are playing with someone else. It will show you the GenQuiz rounds where it is your turn to play. Click on the GenQuiz to begin playing your turn. Play is the same as described in the Challenge a Friend section.

It will show you the GenQuiz rounds that you have completed, which rounds are waiting on your friend and which rounds your friend has also finished. Clicking on one of these rounds will show your score and your friend's score if they have completed it.

From the GenQuiz score page, you can challenge this friend again by clicking the "Challenge Again" button.

Hints!

You may win or purchase hints to help you during a GenQuiz. This page describes each hint and what it does during the game. The little circle on each hint shows how many of those hints you have available to you. A Facebook account is required to store the hints you have won.

One way to win hints is by clicking the "Daily Hint!" button. Clicking this button will check if you have made any recent changes to your family tree. If you have made changes, then you will be awarded a new hint. Each day you make changes to your family tree will allow you win a new hint on that day. This will encourage you to improve your family tree each day. It doesn't matter what change you make, any change will count.

If a hint is available for a question, then that hint will show up under the gauge and question type letter on the GenQuiz question screen. To use a hint, simply click on it and it will be applied to the current question.
