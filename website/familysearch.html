FamilySearch Integration

GenQuizitive and FamilySearch

GenQuizitive downloads data from your FamilySearch Family Tree to use in each game round. It will use the people, places, dates and photos. GenQuizitive is a read-only FamilySearch application and does not make any attempts to update your FamilySearch Family Tree.

Visit GenQuizitive on the Family Search App Gallery

Connecting to FamilySearch

When you first start the application, GenQuizitive will ask you to enter your FamilySearch account credentials to login. GenQuizitive will remember your credentials so that you only have to login once from each device. They are encrypted and secure.

After successfully logging in, GenQuizitive will download data and photos for you, your close relatives, and your ancestors for each game round.
