About GenQuizitive

 

GenQuizitive is an interactive, social, genealogy game where players challenge each other to trivia quizzes about their personal family history. Each GenQuiz consists of a series of trivia questions about your family tree. Questions become more difficult as the round progresses. The player who answers the questions the fastest wins that GenQuiz.
